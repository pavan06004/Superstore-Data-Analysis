# Business Insights

## Overview
The Superstore dataset was analyzed using SQL and Python to understand business performance across sales, customers, products, and regions.

## Key Findings

### Sales
- The West region generated the highest overall sales.
- Technology was the highest-selling product category.
- A small number of products contributed a significant portion of total sales.

### Profit
- Some states generated negative profit despite having high sales.
- Discounts had a noticeable impact on profitability.
- Profit varied considerably across regions and categories.

### Customers
- A few customers contributed a large share of total revenue.
- Customer segments showed different purchasing behavior.
- Identifying high-value customers can support targeted marketing.

### Products
- Technology products generally produced higher revenue.
- Some products had high sales but relatively low profit.
- Product rankings highlighted the best-performing items.

### Overall Conclusion
The analysis demonstrates how SQL and Python can be combined to clean data, perform business analysis, visualize trends, and generate actionable insights for decision-making.
